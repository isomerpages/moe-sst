<p>The Houses has been in existence in the School of Science and Technology,
Singapore (SST) since its inception and has served as an organisational
structure for some of the intra-school competitions.</p>
<p>The objectives of the House system is to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>provide leadership development structure and opportunities for students
who are inclined towards Sports and Wellness</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>support the school and teachers in our Wellness Promotion efforts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>assist in building school culture, creating a positive school identity
and nurturing a sense of fraternity across all levels from Secondary One
to Four through sports and outdoor education activities.</p>
</li>
</ul>
