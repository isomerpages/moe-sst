<p>The Student Council (SC) aims to be the guardians of the school ethos
by building a strong school identity and infusing a sense of school pride.</p>
<h5>Guardian of the School Ethos</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To promote a characteristic spirit of culture and community through our
attitudes and aspirations</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To be dependable and inspire confidence</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>To uphold and set the benchmark for behaviour, inspiring the school population
to follow the lead</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h5>The Bridge Between the School and Student Body</h5>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>To support the school student management programme through preventive
means</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Be in touch with the pulse of the student body to create a better school
experience for all</p>
</li>
</ul>
