Taekwondo is a martial art form that combines both physical training and mental development, inculcating discipline and values in students as they progress through belt levels and advanced sequences (Poomsae). Sparring (Kyorugi) and unarmed combat help improve reaction time, quick thinking and temperament.

SST Taekwondo is committed to helping students develop their skills and character. Under the guidance of coaches Mr. Jeffrey Tan, Mr Stephen Lim and Ms April Teo, from Taistella Sports Pte Ltd, the Taekwondo programme looks forward to bringing forth medal-winning individuals and teams in the National School Games and other competitions.
